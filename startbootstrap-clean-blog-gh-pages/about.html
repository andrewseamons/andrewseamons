About Me

I live for the big moments
Academic & Career Interests

As a marketing major at Brigham Young University, I am passionate about blending creativity with data-driven strategy to build impactful brands and campaigns. I’m particularly interested in consumer behavior, brand development, and digital marketing. My goal is to apply these interests in a business setting that values innovation, collaboration, and integrity.

Athletics & Leadership

Being a student-athlete on the BYU lacrosse team has shaped me into a disciplined and resilient leader. I’ve learned to balance rigorous training with academic excellence, communicate effectively within a team, and perform under pressure. These experiences have taught me how to lead by example, push through adversity, and commit fully to my goals.

Passions & Lifestyle

Outside of school and sports, I'm a true enthusiast of the outdoors and active living. I enjoy skiing fresh powder, golfing with friends, wakesurfing on summer lakes, boating, and exploring new hiking trails. I believe in maintaining a well-rounded lifestyle and am always looking for new adventures that challenge me physically and mentally. My passion for sports and nature fuels my energy, creativity, and drive.
